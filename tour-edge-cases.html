Edge-Case Playbook
8 canonical scenarios (spec §MM)
  1. Item damaged mid-tour — source replacement / sub-rent / redesign
  2. Crew drops out mid-tour — replace from next stop forward
  3. Truck breaks down between stops — leg disrupted, reschedule
  4. Double-booked inventory — tour blocks general availability
  5. Stop cancelled mid-tour — cancellation fee + route adjust
  6. Local hire no-show — field coverage adjust + performance flag
  7. Client changes master ROS — future stops re-clone, past stops archive
  8. Partial shipment — catch-up shipment to next stop
