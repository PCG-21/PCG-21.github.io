Premier Non-Exempt Technician Pay Rules
Consecutive Hour Rules
  • Straight time: first 10 consecutive hours after call start
  • Overtime 1.5×: 10th → 16th consecutive hour OR > 40h/week
  • Double time 2.0×: > 16 consecutive hours
  • Premium time 2.0×: hours worked 12am–6am
10-Hour Call Minimum
  • Applies per event (not per day) — multiple events in 24h = multiple minimums
  • Also applies to out-of-town work, dark days, travel
  • Does not apply to shop/prep/meeting/training (non-event hourly)
Gifted vs PTO for 40h threshold
  • Gifted hours count toward 40h FLSA OT threshold
  • PTO / Holiday / Sick / Bereavement / Jury Duty do not count
Holidays (8 recognized)
  • New Year's Day · Jan 1
  • Memorial Day · last Mon May
  • Independence Day · Jul 4
  • Labor Day · first Mon Sep
  • Thanksgiving · 4th Thu Nov
  • Day after Thanksgiving
  • Christmas Eve · Dec 24
  • Christmas Day · Dec 25
Paid 8h automatically. If worked, 8h holiday + OT on worked hours.
Out-of-Town (> 60mi)
  • 10-hour minimum applies
  • Air travel: 2h before flight, 1h after
  • Vehicle travel: door-to-door
  • Per diem at GSA rate of destination city
  • Dark day (no work) = 10-hour minimum if willing/able
Turnaround & Continuous
  • Continuous (< 4h break): entire 4h paid at current rate
  • Turnaround (< 8h break after ≥10h shift): next shift starts at last rate of pay until 8h break
Work Week
  • Mon 12:00am → Sun 11:59pm
  • Submit by Tuesday 3pm
  • Pay day: Friday
Source: Premier Pay Rules spreadsheet · Non-Exempt Technician. Applies automatically to Show Work / Travel / On Call rows. Shop, Prep, Meeting, and Training rows are paid hourly with no 10-hour minimum.
