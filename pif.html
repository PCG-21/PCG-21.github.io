STEP 1 · PROJECT TYPE

What are you creating?

Each type has different upstream fields and triggers different downstream setup (spec §II vs §2.2 vs §4).
🎤

Show

A single commercial event for a client — one venue, one (or a few) contiguous dates. The default 90% of the time.

Examples: LCE 2026 Conference · Accrue Board Retreat · Microsoft Summer Summit
🗂

Program

A group of related Shows (possibly simultaneous) under one client/budget umbrella. Each sub-show is its own Project, but they share scope and reporting.

Examples: SAE WCX (5 breakout pavilions) · Annual road-show series · ELSO annual circuit
🚌

Tour

Sequential execution — same gear + core crew + ROS travel city-to-city with local adaptations. Requires inventory & crew package, not per-stop allocations.

Examples: Lincoln Navigator National Tour · Stellantis 2026 · Brand roadshows
