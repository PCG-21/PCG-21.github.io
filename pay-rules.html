Pay Rules

Premier non-exempt technician rules that drive the timecard engine (straight / OT / DT / premium / minimums). Admin-only.
ST — Standard Time (base rate)
OT — Overtime (×1.5)
DT — Double Time (×2.0, In-House Union only)
PT — Premium Time (×1.9, IATSE only)
Prevailing Rate: Position remains at the highest rate of pay reached during the prior shift until an 8-hour break from work is received. Applies to both IATSE and In-House Union.

Source of Truth
These settings are applied live by PCG.api.computeTimecardTotals() every time a timecard is computed. Changes here affect future timecard calculations only — approved timecards are locked and remain at the rules that were in effect when approved. Changes are logged to the audit trail under admin.payRules.update. Source document: Premier Pay Rules — Non-Exempt Technician.
