Warehouse Response Loop (§10.6)
  1. PM / TD / TSM submits AO from field
  2. WH Lead gets notification (SMS if emergency)
  3. WH Lead acknowledges — enters ETA
  4. WH Tech picks items, scans OUT
  5. Driver/runner dispatched — manifest created
  6. PM confirms delivery at venue
  7. Items return tracked if returnTracked=true
  8. Finance/AE sets billableDecision at closeout
Origination Rule (§G.4)
Add Orders do NOT originate from general field crew. They are initiated by PM / TD / TSM / AE. Crew tells TSM/PM what's needed — they submit here.
